Why Sustainable Agriculture Matters

Organic farming and animal husbandry help protect nature and improve human health. They reduce pollution, improve soil quality, and provide healthy food for everyone.

Organic Farming

Organic farming avoids chemical fertilizers and pesticides. It improves soil fertility, protects biodiversity, and produces nutritious food.
