Why Sustainable Agriculture?

Sustainable agriculture protects nature while meeting human needs. Organic farming and animal husbandry reduce environmental damage and promote long-term food security.

Organic Farming

Organic farming avoids chemical fertilizers and pesticides. It improves soil fertility, conserves water, and supports biodiversity.

Animal Husbandry

Responsible animal husbandry ensures animal welfare, reduces pollution, and provides safe, nutritious food.
